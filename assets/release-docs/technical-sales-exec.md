## What is Shifter?

A **self-service cyber range platform** that lets you demonstrate AI-driven attacks against live XDR/XSIAM infrastructure in a customer's browser. No setup, no local tools, no infrastructure headaches.

**Key differentiator**: Shows real attacks generating real detections in real-time.

## When to Use Shifter

### ✅ **Perfect Fit**
- XDR/XSIAM active opportunity
- Customer skeptical about detection capabilities
- Technical buyer wants proof of value
- Competitive situation (differentiate with live demo)
- Stalled POC (inject energy with dynamic demonstration)

### ⚠️ **Requires Coordination**
- Customer doesn't have XDR yet (use demo tenant)
- Large audience (schedule in advance, ensure bandwidth)
- Compliance-sensitive environment (emphasize isolation)

### ❌ **Not Appropriate**
- Initial cold outreach (qualify first)
- Non-technical executive audience (use different messaging)
- Customer not interested in security validation

## Demo Best Practices

### Pre-Demo
- ✅ Confirm XDR agent access
- ✅ Test portal login
- ✅ Prepare customer XDR tenant view
- ✅ Set expectations (what they'll see)

### During Demo
- 🎯 Focus on business outcomes, not technical details
- 🎯 Let the AI work (autonomous attack is the feature)
- 🎯 Pause to show XDR detections as they appear
- 🎯 Connect attacks to real-world threat scenarios

### Post-Demo
- 📊 Export detection data for their review
- 📋 Propose next steps (POC, expanded testing)
- 🤝 Introduce specialists if needed
- 📈 Update opportunity in CRM
