**Hint unlocks now enforce the same availability policy as flag submission and reject route/body challenge mismatches.** `ctf.services.hint.use_hint` now applies a shared `assert_challenge_available_for_participant` helper (event match, ACTIVE event, competition window, challenge visibility, release state, prerequisites) so participants cannot retrieve hint text for hidden, locked, unreleased, or out-of-window challenges, or for challenges in other events. `api_use_hint` additionally verifies that the URL's `challenge_id` and the optional body `hint_id` refer to the same challenge, closing a path where a participant could unlock a different challenge's hint by supplying its UUID.
