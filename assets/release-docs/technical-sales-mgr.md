## What is Shifter?

A **self-service cyber range platform** that enables your technical team to demonstrate AI-driven attacks against XDR/XSIAM-protected infrastructure. Think "demo-as-a-service" for security validation.

**Business impact**: Faster demos, higher conversion rates, more efficient SE utilization.

## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: "SE doesn't have XDR access"
**Solution**: Use demo tenant, partner with customer's team, or focus on prospects with existing XDR

### Challenge: "Demo feels too technical for executive audience"
**Solution**: Adjust messaging—focus on business risk, not attack details. Shorter demo, more discussion.

### Challenge: "Customer wants custom scenario we haven't practiced"
**Solution**: AI is flexible—work with SE team to develop new scenarios, document for future use

### Challenge: "Range provisioning fails"
**Solution**: Fallback plan—use recorded demo, reschedule, or escalate to Shifter support
