About SCM Credentials
In SCM, go to Settings > Registration > Registration PIN. Create a new PIN if needed. The folder name, PIN ID, and PIN value are shown after creation.
About Deployment Profiles
A deployment profile contains your VM-Series authcode from the Palo Alto Networks Customer Support Portal. This authcode is used to license NGFWs during provisioning. Each authcode can typically license multiple instances depending on your agreement.
