Browser SSH terminals now have per-process and per-user session caps, idle and maximum-duration timeouts, and a low-frequency output poll, so terminal websocket load no longer destabilizes the portal during live events. The limits are tunable via the `TERMINAL_MAX_SESSIONS`, `TERMINAL_MAX_SESSIONS_PER_USER`, `TERMINAL_IDLE_TIMEOUT_SECONDS`, `TERMINAL_MAX_SESSION_SECONDS`, and `TERMINAL_READ_POLL_SECONDS` environment variables.
