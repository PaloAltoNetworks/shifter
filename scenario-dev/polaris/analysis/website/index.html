§ 04 — MISSION FUNNEL

Attempted → touched (≥1 solve) → fully cleared. The gap between "attempted" and "touched" is the frustration pocket. M5 Bunker is pure frustration — 7 participants attempted it, 0 ever scored.

§ 05 — THE CEILING

Four operators tied at 4,850 points. Curiously, every one of them solved the exact same 49 challenges — zero divergence. Everything except M5 Bunker. The ceiling wasn't about operator skill at the top; it was a structural wall.
