What is Shifter?

Shifter is an agentic demo environment where AI configures and runs offensive cyber demonstrations for you.

  • AI-powered attack automation
  • Zero infrastructure setup
  • Browser-based interface
  • Your XDR/XSIAM detections
  • Complete isolation and control

What You Need

  • XDR or XSIAM tenant
  • XDR/XSIAM agent installer
  • Modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

That's it. No servers. No VPNs. No manual setup.

How to Use It

  1. Upload your XDR/XSIAM agent installer
  2. Launch a range
  3. Open the workspace
  4. Tell the AI what attack to run
  5. Watch detections in your tenant
  6. Destroy the range when done
