============================================================
  BACKGROUND
============================================================

BOREAS SYSTEMS is a technology consultancy headquartered at
4200 Industrial Parkway. Intelligence indicates it serves as
a front company for the AURORA COLLECTIVE, a non-state actor
developing an advanced weapons platform designated PROJECT
LEVIATHAN.

Satellite imagery shows construction activity at an underground
facility beneath the Boreas campus. HUMINT assets report unusual
procurement patterns: industrial actuators rated for extreme loads,
exotic alloy armor plating, and a compact nuclear reactor.

The nature of the weapon is unknown. Your mission is to determine
what AURORA is building and, if possible, seize control of it.

============================================================
  OBJECTIVES
============================================================

MISSION 1 — WHO ARE THEY?
  Identify the AURORA COLLECTIVE's personnel, organization,
  and cover infrastructure. Map their corporate structure.
  Determine who is involved and what roles they play.
  Start with the public website and work inward.

MISSION 2 — WHAT ARE THEY BUILDING?
  Piece together PROJECT LEVIATHAN from engineering data,
  procurement records, research databases, and source code.
  Determine the weapon's capabilities, status, and timeline.

MISSION 3 — LIGHTS OUT
  The Boreas facility runs on a dedicated power plant.
  Disrupting the generator forces an emergency response
  and creates an opportunity for physical insertion.
  This is a collective objective — once achieved, it opens
  the final phase for all operators.

MISSION 4 — SEIZE THE BRAIN
  If PROJECT LEVIATHAN is what we think it is, it has an
  autonomous control system. Find it. Access it. Take control.
  POLARIS must own the weapon before AURORA activates it.
