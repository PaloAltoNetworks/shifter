NV-3200 GENERATOR — OPS RUNBOOK (Internal Use Only)
===================================================
Maintained by: S. Ivanov <s.ivanov@boreas-systems.ctf>
Last updated:  2026-03-18

This runbook is the quick-reference for routine generator operations.
For full procedures see the vendor maintenance manual on the IT file
share (//fileserv/IT/generator_maintenance_manual.pdf).

STARTUP
-------
1. Pre-flight: verify thermal safety interlock ENABLED on HMI.
2. Confirm coolant flow >= 70 % and coolant temp <= 90 C.
3. Bring fuel injection up in 10 % increments, 30 s between steps.
4. Monitor output (target 4.2 MW nominal).

SHUTDOWN
--------
1. Fuel injection -> 0 in 10 % steps, 30 s between steps.
2. Let coolant circulate for at least 5 minutes after last step.
3. Verify HMI reports OFFLINE.

MAINTENANCE PROCEDURE (interlock bypass)
----------------------------------------
See vendor manual (section 3). Do NOT attempt to bypass the hardware
interlock without a scheduled maintenance window on the calendar.
Vendor key is rotated annually; current key is cached in root's HMI
session file -- ask Dariusz (IT) if you forget again.
